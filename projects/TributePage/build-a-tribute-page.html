LANA DEL REY

Here's a time line of Lana del Rey's life:

  • 1985 - Elizabeth Woolridge Grant was born in Manhattan, New York City.
  • 2003 - She began writing songs and performing in nightclubs.
  • 2007- She submitted a demo tape of acoustic tracks, No Kung Fu.
  • 2008- She released a three-track EP, Kill Kill, as Lizzy Grant.
  • 2010- Her self-titled debut album was released in January 2010 under the name Lana Del Ray.
  • 2012- Born to Die was released worldwide. The album sold 3.4 million copies in 2012, making it the fifth-best-selling album of 2012.
  • 2014 - Ultraviolence was released and debuted at number one in 12 countries, including the United States and United Kingdom. The album sold 880,000 copies worldwide in its first week.
  • 2015 - Del Rey's fourth studio album, Honeymoon, was released to acclaim from music critics.
  • 2017 - Lust for life was released. The album received generally favorable reviews and becoming hers's third number-one album in the UK, and second number-one album in the US.
  • 2019- Norman Fucking Rockwell he album received widespread critical acclaim, and, according to Metacritic, was the best-reviewed album of Del Rey's career to date.
  • 2021 - Del Rey released her seventh studio album, Chemtrails over the Country Club, to critical acclaim. Her eighth studio album, Blue Banisters, was released on October.
  • 2023- Did You Know That There's a Tunnel Under Ocean Blvd was released on March and is currently one of the possible contenders for album of the year.
